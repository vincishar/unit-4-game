Crystal Collector

How to play the game

  1. A random number is given at the start of the game."Number To Get"
  2. There are four crystals below.
    • Blue
    • Green
    • Red
    • Yellow
  3. When clicking on a crystal, you will add an amount of points to your total score.
  4. You win the game by matching your total score to the mixed variable.
  5. You lose the game if your total score goes beyond the number.
  6. The points of each crystal is hidden from you, until clicking.
  7. Once the game starts, it will change the values of each crystal.
